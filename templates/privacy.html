Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

When you scan a website, we store: the URL you entered, the scan results (grades and technical findings), and a timestamp. If you purchase a full report, Stripe processes your payment — we don't store credit card details.

What We Don't Collect

We don't require account creation, we don't track your browsing across other sites, and we don't sell data to third parties.

Analytics

We use Umami, a privacy-focused analytics tool. It collects anonymous page view data with no cookies and no personal tracking.

How Scanning Works

SiteGrade makes standard HTTP requests, SSL connections, and DNS queries to the website you specify — the same type of requests any web browser or command-line tool would make. No invasive testing, no penetration testing, no vulnerability scanning.

Questions? Email tinyship.ai@gmail.com.
