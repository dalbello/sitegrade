Frequently Asked Questions

What does SiteGrade check?

SiteGrade runs 7 real-time checks against your website: SSL certificate validity, security headers, page performance (TTFB, size, compression), tech stack detection, DNS health (including SPF/DMARC), mobile readiness, and calculates a weighted overall grade from A+ to F.

Is the scan free?

Yes! The scan and grade preview are completely free. You can see your A-F grade across all 6 categories at no cost. The full detailed report with specific findings, fix recommendations, and a downloadable PDF is $1.99.

How is the overall grade calculated?

The overall grade is a weighted average: SSL (25%), Security Headers (20%), Performance (20%), DNS (15%), Mobile (10%), Tech Stack (10%). Scores of 90+ get A+, 80+ get A, 70+ get B, and so on down to F.

Can I share the report with my client or boss?

Absolutely — that's what the PDF is for. The $1.99 full report generates a professional, branded PDF with grades, detailed findings, and specific recommendations you can email to stakeholders.

Does SiteGrade use AI?

No. SiteGrade performs real network analysis — actual SSL handshakes, live HTTP requests, DNS resolution, and HTML parsing. No AI, no estimates, no guessing. Every data point comes from testing your site directly.

How long does a scan take?

Typically 5-15 seconds depending on the target site's response time. SSL checks, DNS resolution, and HTTP requests all happen in parallel where possible.
